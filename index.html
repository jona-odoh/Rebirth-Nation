About Us

Welcome to The Rebirth Nation

There has never been a time like now when we feel the pangs of the earth. Yet a little light flickers in the distance, its presence putting the darkness to flight.

God is raising a people ready for His move, riding on the waves of His presence and bringing revival to the nations. Our hearts beat to see all that God has spoken over the nations accomplished.

"A kingdom of Sons. To them the nations are given, through them the nations are reborn."

- Daniel Adagiri, Lead Minister

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"A people raised to see the nations rebirthed."

The Rebirth Nation is established by the authority of God and His word to reach and raise a people for God who ultimately will rebirth entire nations into their redemptive purposes.

01

PRAY

Rebirthing nations into their Redemptive purposes by grace through intercessory prayer. We believe that God is still rebirthing nations in our day as promised in the prophetic scriptures.

Isaiah 66:7-9, Isaiah 62:18c
02

PREACH/TEACH

Restoring the dignity of humanity by revealing man's true identity in God. Through the gospel, humanity will come into complete knowledge of Sonship in Christ.

Ephesians 2:15, Isaiah 61:1-5
03

PLANT

Raising & training people in strong and close-knitted communities that will establish the kingdom of God and expand its frontiers to the ends of the earth.

Isaiah 9:7, Acts 1:8, Matthew 28:19-20
What We Stand For

Our Values

STEWARDSHIP

We are stewards of all the graces the Lord has given to us. We faithfully use and manage every resource to accomplish the Lord's commands.

OUTREACH

Our arms will always be stretched out to touch the unreached. Every program is organized to win the lost and disciple them.

NOBILITY

Royalty is our heritage as children of God. Our conduct and ministry exemplify the standards of the kingdom. Excellence is key.

THE SUPERNATURAL

Our life is supernatural. We go for the impossible on the foundations of faith, prayer, prophecy & the presence of Jesus.
